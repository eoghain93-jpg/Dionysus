1   Logging In

1
Open the till on the screen. You will see a keypad asking for your PIN.
2
Type your 4-digit PIN and press Enter. Your name will appear at the top of the screen. You are now logged in.
If you type the wrong PIN, press the back arrow to clear it and try again.

2   Taking a Sale (Cash or Card)

This is what you will do most of the time.

1
Tap the drinks or food the customer wants. Each button you tap adds one item to the order. You will see it appear on the right-hand side of the screen.
2
Check the order on the right looks correct. The total is shown at the bottom in large numbers.
3
Tell the customer the total.
4
Press Cash or Card. For card — the machine will handle the rest. For cash — see Section 4 below.
5
The receipt will print automatically. The screen clears and is ready for the next customer.

Finding the right drink

Use the buttons at the top to switch between drink types:

Button What it shows
AllEvery product
DraughtPints and half pints from the taps
BottleBottled beers and ciders
SpiritSpirits and measures
SoftSoft drinks and mixers
WineWine (glass and bottle)
FoodFood items
If you see a small orange dot on a product button, the stock is running low. Tell the manager.
If you see a yellow "PROMO" badge on a product, that item is on promotion and the discounted price is already showing.

3   Taking a Sale on a Tab

A tab lets a customer pay at the end rather than each round.

1
Find the customer in the search box at the top. Type their first or last name. Tap their name when it appears.
2
Their name will appear in the order panel on the right. A blue bar will show their name at the top of the order.
3
Add the drinks as normal, then press Tab. The order is added to their account. Nothing is charged yet.
You can only press Tab if a customer has been selected. The Tab button will not work without a name linked to the order.

To settle a tab later, go to the Tabs page from the menu and press Settle next to their name.

4   Giving Change (Cash)

1
Press Cash after building the order. A box will appear asking how much the customer gave you.
2
Type in the amount the customer handed you. For example: if they gave you a £20 note, type 20.
3
The screen will show how much change to give back.
4
Press Confirm to finish and open the cash drawer.

5   Removing or Changing Items

To add more of the same item

Tap the product button again — it will increase the quantity by 1 each time.

Or press the + button next to the item in the order panel.

To reduce the quantity

Press the – button next to the item in the order panel.

To remove an item completely

Press the X (remove) button next to the item.

If the order is wrong and you want to start again, remove all the items one by one or press each item's X button.

6   Members-Only Event Mode

On members-only event days (sport finals, members' nights, etc.), the manager may turn on Event Mode. While Event Mode is active, every sale automatically uses member pricing — you do not need to identify each customer individually.

How to tell if Event Mode is on

A bright green banner runs across the top of the till that says MEMBERS PRICING — EVENT MODE. Every product tile shows the member price. Every sale is charged at member pricing automatically.

Turning Event Mode on or off

1
Tap the toggle at the top of the till. When Event Mode is off it shows as a small grey button: "Start members-only event mode". When on, it shows as the big green banner with an "End event mode" button on the right.
2
Confirm in the prompt that appears. You'll be asked "Start event mode?" or "End event mode?" — tap to confirm.
Only turn Event Mode on or off if the manager has told you to. It changes the price of every sale on the till.
Event Mode automatically turns OFF when you log out or change shift. The next person to sign in starts with it off.
Tabs still need a member: Event Mode only changes prices, not who the sale is linked to. To put a sale on someone's tab, you still have to find them in the search box first.

7   Giving Cashback

Cashback means a customer pays by card but also gets physical cash back. It is not a loyalty reward.

1
Press Cashback below the category buttons.
2
Follow the instructions on screen to enter the amount.
3
Give the customer the cash from the till.
Only give cashback if the manager has told you this is allowed. Always record it properly — do not skip this step.

8   Recording Prize Wins

The pub's two fruit machines pay out paper vouchers. When a member brings a voucher to the bar to claim, you record the prize win and pay out the cash from the till.

1
Press Prize Win below the category buttons.
2
Enter the voucher amount in £. For example: £5.00 voucher → type 5.00.
3
Tap which machine the voucher came from. Choose Machine 1 or Machine 2 — the supplier reimburses for each machine separately, so this matters.
4
Press Record. The cash drawer will open. Pay the customer the cash amount equal to the voucher value.
5
Put the voucher in the till drawer for safekeeping. The till has already recorded the prize win against the machine for supplier reimbursement — keeping the voucher in the drawer just keeps it safe.
When you reconcile cash at end of day, do NOT count vouchers as cash. The till tracks them separately, so you only count actual notes and coins.

9   Logging Wastage

Wastage means a drink was spilled, broken, or had to be thrown away.

1
Press Wastage below the category buttons.
2
Tap the product that was wasted.
3
Confirm the quantity and press Confirm.
Always log wastage — it helps the manager track stock accurately.

10   Staff Drinks

If you are given a complimentary drink by the manager, you must log it.

1
Press Staff Drink below the category buttons.
2
Tap the drink that was given.
3
Confirm and press Done.
Do not use this unless the manager has approved the drink. Every entry is recorded and reviewed.

11   End of Day

The staff member closing the pub runs the end-of-day. Don't wait for the manager — if you're locking up, you do the close.

1
Go to the Reports page using the menu at the top.
2
Press Z Report. This shows the end-of-day sales summary on screen.
3
Count the cash in the till and enter the totals. Enter the opening float and the actual cash counted. The till will work out the variance against the expected amount.
4
Check the Tabs page for any open tabs. Settle anything that needs to be cleared before close.
5
Press Close Day when everything reconciles. This locks the till for the day and emails the report to the manager automatically. The next staff member will need to log back in to start a new day.
If the variance is more than a few pounds out, double-check your count before pressing Close Day. Note any anomaly somewhere the manager can see it.

12   Common Problems

Try the steps in What to try first. If something is still broken after that, raise it in the EPOS WhatsApp chat — that's the fastest way to get a fix.

Problem What to try If still stuck
My PIN is not working Press the back arrow to clear. Try again — make sure you're tapping the right digits. PINs are 4 digits. Manager (only they can reset a PIN)
The receipt is not printing 1. Check the printer has paper.
2. Check the printer is plugged in and the green light is on.
3. Go to Settings and press Test Print.
The sale has still gone through — the customer has been charged. Take payment as normal.		 EPOS WhatsApp chat
The drawer didn't open after a cash sale 1. Make sure the printer is plugged in (the drawer is wired through it).
2. Go to Settings and press Open Drawer to open it manually.		 EPOS WhatsApp chat
"Print failed" message appears The sale has been recorded — keep serving. Try Settings → Test Print to confirm the printer's working again. EPOS WhatsApp chat if it keeps happening
The screen is frozen or not responding 1. Wait 10 seconds — it might catch up.
2. Pull down on the screen to refresh the page.
3. As a last resort, close the till tab and reopen club-epos.vercel.app.		 EPOS WhatsApp chat
The Tab button is greyed out Search for and select the member first using the search bar at the top. Tab will then unlock. (Not a fault — that's how it's designed.)
The till is showing member prices to non-members Event Mode is on. Look for the green banner at the top — tap End event mode and confirm. Manager if you're not sure whether it should be on
I forgot to record a prize win Record it now using the Prize Win button. The voucher is already in the till drawer — leave it there. (Self-fix.)
I charged the wrong amount Tell the next customer briefly to wait. Note the order details (member name if any, time, items). Manager — only they can refund and re-ring
I pressed the wrong payment button (e.g. Cash instead of Card) Note the order time, total, and what should have been pressed. Manager — they correct the payment record
A product is missing from the till Take payment as a similar product if the price is the same. Note the product name on a piece of paper. Manager — only they can add products
Always raise persistent technical problems in the EPOS WhatsApp chat after you've tried the steps above. Don't wait until end of shift — the longer something is broken, the more sales it costs.
